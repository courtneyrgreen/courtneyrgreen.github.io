Research · 2024
Probabilistic Modeling of Prosecutorial Misconduct and Over-Policing in Chicago
✦ 2025 Georgetown University Merit Scholarship recipient, awarded through anonymous faculty review.
Chicago has led the nation in exonerations for five consecutive years. This project investigates why, drawing on Illinois arrest records from the Illinois Criminal Justice Information Authority, exoneration and misconduct data, and incarceration demographics. Through geospatial mapping, disproportionality indexes, and clustering, the analysis traces how perjury and evidence suppression disproportionately drive wrongful convictions.
