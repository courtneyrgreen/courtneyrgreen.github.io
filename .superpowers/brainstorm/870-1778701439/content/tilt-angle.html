How steeply is the orbital ring tilted?

The tilt determines how "3D" the ring feels — shallow is subtle, steep is dramatic

15° tilt — nearly flat

A — Shallow (15°)

Subtle tilt. Ring feels like a gently inclined orbit, nearly overhead view.

25° tilt — clear 3D

B — Medium (25°)

Clearly 3D. Ring reads as an inclined orbit — like an orrery at eye level.

38° tilt — dramatic

C — Steep (38°)

Maximum drama. Front planet surges forward, back planet recedes sharply upward.
